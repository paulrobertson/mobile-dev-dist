Privacy Policy

mySavings

Last updated: 19th April, 2026

1. Overview
This Privacy Policy describes how this app ("the App") handles your information.

The App is designed to respect your privacy. It does not collect, store, or share personal data with external servers or third parties.
2. Data Collection
The App does not collect any personal data.

All information you enter into the App, including financial details such as:
  • account names
  • bank names
  • balances
  • notes or categories
is stored only on your device and, if enabled, securely in your personal iCloud account.

This data is:
  • not accessible by the developer
  • not transmitted to any external servers
  • not used for tracking or analytics
3. Data Storage
Your data is stored in the following ways:
  • On-device storage: Data is stored locally on your device.
  • iCloud sync (optional): If enabled, your data is securely stored in your personal iCloud account and synced across your Apple devices.
All iCloud data is managed by Apple and subject to Apple's privacy and security policies.
4. Analytics
The App uses App Store-provided analytics only, supplied by Apple.

These analytics:
  • are aggregated and anonymised
  • do not identify you personally
  • are used only to understand general app usage
The App does not use third-party analytics tools.
5. Tracking
The App does not track users.
  • No tracking across apps or websites
  • No advertising identifiers are used
  • No data is sold or shared
6. In-App Purchases
The App may offer optional in-app purchases to unlock additional features.

Payments are processed securely by Apple. The developer does not receive or store your payment details.
7. Notifications
The App may request permission to send notifications.
  • Notifications are optional and can be enabled or disabled in the App or device settings
  • No personal data is collected or transmitted through notifications
8. Data Sharing
The App does not share any data with third parties.

All data remains under your control on your device and within your iCloud account.
9. Data Deletion
You can delete your data at any time by:
  • using in-app data deletion features, or
  • uninstalling the App
If iCloud sync is enabled, deleting data within the App will also remove it from your iCloud storage.
10. Children's Privacy
The App is not intended for children under the age of 13.

The App does not knowingly collect any personal data from children.
11. Changes to This Policy
This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.

Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
