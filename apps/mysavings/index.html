iOS App

mySavings

Your savings data in one place

Track savings balances across multiple banks and accounts, stay ahead of maturity and bonus-rate expiry dates, and visualize your money through clear charts and tables.

I built mySavings to solve a personal problem: keeping track of multiple savings accounts across different banks, each with their own maturity dates and bonus rate expiries.

I wanted a simple way to see all my balances in one place, get reminders about important dates, and understand how my savings were growing over time.

mySavings is the result of that need, designed to be a clean and focused tool for anyone looking to manage their personal savings more effectively.
